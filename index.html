ABOUT US

Welcome to APM Sea Services (APMSS) one of the best ship suppliers and ship chandelling services in Visakhapatnam, Gangavaram, Kakinada and Paradip in East Coast. APMSS has the resources and expertise to provide our clients in shipping industry with complete satisfaction for all the service including deck stores, engine stores and provisions. We have a Staff of experienced mariners in our company.

We are best suited to supply any kind of off shore and ocean - going vessels. We take the utmost pride in supplying our customers with the highest level of services possible and we are proud of our ability to meet their requirements on time and within the budget.

We has served several shipping companies, domestic and International with extremely high quality and standards. We have always exceeded the expectations of the companies we worked with. We strive to be the best in the industry by providing excellent services with competitive prices and not compromising on standards within the reach and budget of the companies.

Our Team consists of 3 members and committed to meet the client /Customer needs by providing appropriate Quality services round the Clock. We source the Customer requirement with the best quality available in our area as well as to find and supply the Organic Farming commodities at reasonable prices without hesitation.

APM Sea Services (APMSS) are located on the eastern costal city Visakhapatnam (City of Destiny) in the state of Andhra Pradesh, India. Established by an experienced shipping agent in Sri Gayatri Shipping and Logistics (P) LTD. Over a decades of shipping experience and by understanding the needs and requirements of the Shipping industry and we are tie-up with many shipping companies locally.
